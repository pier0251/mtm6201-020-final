WHO IS JONATHAN

Jonathan Pierre discovered his passion for photography and video during his high school years at Pierre Savard. It all began when he joined the school's media club, where he learned the basics of photography and video production. His interest quickly grew as he experimented with different camera techniques and editing software. During his time at Pierre Savard, Jonathan had the opportunity to work on various projects, including documenting school events, creating promotional videos, and capturing moments that reflected the spirit of his school community.
